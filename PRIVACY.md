Privacy Policy for Phishing Shield Enterprise
This extension is developed as an academic/research project.

Data Collection: The extension only reads the URLs of the websites you visit (Web History) in real-time to analyze them for phishing threats.
Data Usage: URLs are sent securely to our custom Machine Learning API to calculate a risk score.
Data Storage: We do not store, log, or sell your browsing history. The analysis is instantaneous and the data is discarded immediately after the prediction is returned. No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is collected.
